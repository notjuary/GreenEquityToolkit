What does the sustainability tool do?

Create sustainable, equitable and powerful machine learning models. Our tool helps you optimize energy efficiency, promote equity, and prevent discrimination, for truly cutting-edge ML models.

The tool may be able to help you to monitor energy aspects, enabling you to improve more the interaction between energy efficiency and other controls to realize of efficient and environmentally sustainable ML models. In addition to considering aspects of environmental sustainability, the tool can help you to operationalize equity and avoid all forms of discrimination, consolidating more into your ML systems so that they are fair, equitable, moreover efficient.
