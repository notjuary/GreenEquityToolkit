DistilBERT is a "distilled" version of BERT that is smaller and faster, but still retains a good deal of the performance of the original BERT model. This makes it an ideal choice for applications where computational efficiency is important. Despite its smaller size, DistilBERT has shown comparable performance to BERT on a number of natural language processing tasks, including text classification, which is the main task of the GLUE MRPC dataset. DistilBERT requires fewer resources for training and inference than larger models such as BERT, which means that it also yields less CO2
